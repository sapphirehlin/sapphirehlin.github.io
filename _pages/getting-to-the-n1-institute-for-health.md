**Address:** \
28 Medical Drive \
Centre for Life Sciences (CeLS) Building \
Singapore 117456

**Google Maps Directions:** <https://maps.app.goo.gl/PMbqKvHkyzeqr1B96>

The institute is located within the **National University of Singapore (Kent Ridge Campus),** near the **Yong Loo Lin School of Medicine / NUH area.**

## 1. By MRT

The nearest MRT station is **Kent Ridge (CC24)** on the Circle Line. From the MRT station, it will take an approximately 8–10mins walk to reach the (CeLS) Building.

1. Exit Kent Ridge MRT via **Exit A** to reach the **NUH Main Building Lobby**
2. Turn left and follow the sheltered walkway outside the National University Hospital (NUH) Main Building.

	<img class="image-base" src="/images/n1-dir/n1-directions-01.png" />

	<img class="image-base" src="/images/n1-dir/n1-directions-02.png" />

3. Walk past Lobby G and continue toward the **Kopitiam** food court.

	<img class="image-base" src="/images/n1-dir/n1-directions-03.png" />

4. Cross the zebra crossing and follow the slope up **Medical Drive** (keep the hospital buildings on your left).

	<img class="image-base" src="/images/n1-dir/n1-directions-04.png" />

5. The N.1 Institute For Health is located further up the road, near the intersection of Medical Drive and Science Drive 2.

	<img class="image-base" src="/images/n1-dir/n1-directions-05.png" />

6. Follow the pathway up to the building, take the stairs up to the main lobby.

	<img class="image-base" src="/images/n1-dir/n1-directions-06.png" />

	<img class="image-base" src="/images/n1-dir/n1-directions-07.png" />

## 2. By Bus

You may take the following public buses that serve the NUS / NUH area:

### A. Primary Public Bus Services

- **Service 95:** This is the most direct public bus. It stops at the NUH main building and the Kent Ridge MRT station. From the NUH bus stop, it is a short walk up the slope of Medical Drive to the Institute.
- **Services 92, 151, and 200:** These services stop near Kent Ridge MRT Station (Exit A) on Lower Kent Ridge Road.
- **Services 97, 197, 198, and 963:** These stop along the **Ayer Rajah Expressway (AYE)** near NUH. 

Refer to walking directions above from **NUH Main Building Lobby.**

### B. NUS Internal Shuttle Buses (ISB)

If you are already within the National University of Singapore (NUS) campus, you can use the internal shuttle system:

- **Shuttle A1, D2, and K:** These stop at various points around the Kent Ridge campus and the hospital area.

Once you alight at the National University Hospital (NUH) bus stop, look for the slope leading up to **Medical Drive**. Refer to walking directions above from **NUH Main Building Lobby**.

## 3. By Car or Taxi / Private Hire

**Drop-off Point:** Input **28 Medical Drive** into your GPS. The drop-off is located directly in front of the building entrance.

- **Driving Directions:**
	1. From the **AYE (Ayer Rajah Expressway)**, take **Exit 8** (South Buona Vista Road).
	2. Head toward the National University Hospital (NUH).
	3. Turn into **Lower Kent Ridge Road**, then turn into **Medical Drive**.

		<img class="image-base" src="/images/n1-dir/n1-directions-08.png" />

		<img class="image-base" src="/images/n1-dir/n1-directions-09.png" />

- **Parking:** The nearest visitor parking is at **NUS Car Park 10 (CP10)** or the **NUH Medical Centre (Zone B)**. Please note that parking charges apply and lots can be limited during peak office hours.
